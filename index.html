Rockets are Awesome.

Well, that's what we think at least. It is what has brought us together to focus on the creation and development of all things rocketry.

Learn More

Fostering a community

Whether it be the creation of a hobby rocket or the large scale devlopment necessary for intercollegiate competitions, the Vancouver Rocketry Society aims to unite the community towards a central goal of helping the next generation of rocket scientists, engineers and enthusiasts.

Forming Connections

The Vancouver Rocketry Society works closely with UBC Rocket in the testing and development of rockets, and aspires to form similar bonds with other rocketry-related groups throughout greater Vancouver.

Building Something Amazing

At the end of the day, the Vancouver Rocketry Society is about trying to find the most incredible and technologically advanced ways of hurdling an earth bound object up sky high. If that sounds interesting then you're in the right place!
